Mission

The Mission of The 3.5% is to promote the organization of local civics clubs to stand with marginalized communities in the pursuit of human rights and equity for all.


What does accomplishing our Mission look like?

The 3.5% seek to achieve this outcome through the organization of the general public to fund and participate in local civics clubs. We seek to connect organizations to individuals and fundraise for peaceful local action groups and mutual aid organizations.
