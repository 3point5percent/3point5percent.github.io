Mission

The Mission of The 3.5% is to promote the organization of local civics clubs to stand with marginalized communities in the pursuit of human rights for all.


What does accomplishing our Mission look like?

I think the idealist Franklin Delano Roosevelt outlined success well when he announced to the U.S. and Allied Nations the Four Freedoms that they must uphold in the conflict with facism.

  • Freedom of Speech
  • Freedom of Worship
  • Freedom from Want
  • Freedom from Fear

The 3.5% seek to achieve this outcome through the organization of the general public to fund and participate in local civics groups. We seek to connect organizations to individuals and fundraise for peaceful local action groups leading their communities in the areas of freedom of speech, freedom of worship, freedom from want, and freedom from fear.
